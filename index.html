Bio

Hello, I am Mubashwir, a second year Ph.D. student at Wright State University under the supervision of Dr. Keke Chen. My research interest is the System and Software Privacy. Recently, I am working on Mitigating Side-Channel Attacks on Hardware-assisted Trusted Computing. Besides I am Collaborating Data Visualization Project funded by NIH.

Prior to that, I was working at Samsung R&D for 5+ years. Involved in several research and commercial projects. During that time I grew my expertise in Development in Linux Environment, IoT and Bluetooth Low Energy. I also worked in the Android Framework for prototyping research projects. And worked in iOS app development for Samsung Galaxy watch Manager.
