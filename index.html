I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student at Marquette University. I am being advised by Dr. Keke Chen. My research focuses on System Security and Privacy. Currently, I am working on Mitigating Side-Channel Attacks on Hardware-assisted Trusted Computing.

Before starting my Ph.D., I was working at Samsung R&D for 5+ years. At samsung, I was involved in several R&D projects. During that time I grew my expertise in System and Software Architecture, Development in Linux Environment, IoT and Bluetooth Low Energy. I worked in the Android Framework as well for prototyping research projects. Towards the end, I designed and developed the connection module of the Samsung Wearable SDK and partially developed the connection module of Galaxy Watch Manager (iOS version).
